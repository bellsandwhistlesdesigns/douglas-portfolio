St. Thomas Moore

Gymnasium AV Upgrade

Electro-Voice Speakers and Dynacord Amplifiers

Overview

Led a team to upgrade current AV systems throughout the gymnasium. This project required consistent planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of an AV system within a short timeframe.

My Role

  • Planned and coordinated the installation of all new equipment
  • Oversaw seismic securing of rigged speakers
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and installation quality control
  • Troubleshot issues related to new system configurations

Scope of Work

  • Rigged and installed Electro-Voice speakers from the rafters with seismic restraints in place
  • Planned installation phases for all new equipment
  • Reconfigured existing AV rack space to accommodate new amplifiers
  • Planned and coordinated AV installation
  • Completed final installation and system integration

Technical Execution

The gymnasium required a new sound system, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All devices were documented to ensure accurate network integration and long-term maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced all site protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed work according to specifications
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication

Outcome

Successfully delivered a new AV system deployment for the facility. The project was completed to a high standard, with strong documentation practices and seamless coordination.
