St. Thomas Moore

Gymnasium AV Upgrade

Electro-Voice Speakers and Dynacord Amplifiers

Overview

Led a team to upgrade currrent AV throughout the gymnasium. This project required consistent planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of an AV system in a short amount of time.

My Role

  • Plan and coordinate the installation of all new products
  • Seismic control of rigged speakers
  • Manage task tracking, documentation, and quality control of installation
  • Troubleshoot any issues that may arise in new product configurations

Scope of Work

  • Rig and install EV speakers from the rafters with seismic controls in place
  • Plan for stages for installation of all new products
  • Make room in current AV rack for amplifiers
  • Plan and coordinate AV installation
  • Confirm final installation and system incorporation

Technical Execution

The gymnasium required a new sound system, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All new devices were documented, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced all safety protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed their work to written specifications
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication.

Outcome

Successfully delivered new AV deployment for this facility. The project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination.
