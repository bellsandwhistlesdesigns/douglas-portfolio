TransLink | Head Office Relocation

Head office move from Surrey to New Westminster

Overview

Led a four man team to relocate the entire AV configuration. This project required early-stage planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of a very complex AV system in a short amount of time.

My Role

  • Plan and coordinate the intallation of the relocated product in new office to specifications
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and quality control of installers
  • Troubleshoot any issues that may arise in new product configurations

Scope of Work

  • Demo and document all AV products from each office in Surrey,B.C.
  • Planned transfer of all products
  • Planned and coordinated AV intallation for the new head office
  • Final installation and system integration post-construction
  • Ensure configuration of a complex multi-use AV system

Technical Execution

The new office required a detailed AV configuration, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All new devices were documented, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced site protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed their work to written specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale AV deployment for this new facility. The project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination.
