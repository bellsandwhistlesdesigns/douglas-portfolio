Tasty Indian Bistro Abbotsford

AV Systems Lead | Full Restaurant Deployment

Overview

Led the full audiovisual deployment for the new Tasty Indian Bistro in Abbotsford, coordinating installation and project delivery for opening day. This project required early-stage infrastructure planning, cross-trade collaboration, and precise execution of a complex AV system.

My Role

  • Led a team of 4 AV installers with 6 electrical subcontractors
  • Planned and coordinated AV layouts for the restaurant
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and quality control

Scope of Work

  • Full AV installation of new restaurant
  • 6 displays, projector and screen, speakers and subwoofers
  • Extensive pre-wiring during construction phase for all AV infrastructure
  • Final installation and system integration post-construction
  • Restaurant configuration of a complex Crestron AV system

Technical Execution

The restaurant required detailed AV configurations, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All devices were documented, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held weekly safety meetings and enforced site safety protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers, including contractors completed the work to site designs and planning
  • Maintained workflow across changing construction conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale AV deployment for this new restaurant. The project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination across trades.
