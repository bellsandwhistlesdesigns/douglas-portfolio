Auditoriums and Classrooms

Overview

Led a team of 5 to upgrade currrent AV in both school auditoriums. This project required early-stage planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of complex AV systems in a short amount of time.

My Role

  • Plan and coordinate the decommission of old AV in auditoriums
  • Plan and coodinate the installation of monitors, drop down projector, speakers, microphones, and stage lighting
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and quality control of installers
  • Troubleshoot any issues that may arise in new product configurations

Scope of Work

  • Decommission and document all AV products from both auditoriums
  • Planned stages for installation of all new products
  • Planned and coordinated AV intallation
  • Final installation and system integration
  • Ensure complete configuration of a very complex multi-use AV system
  • Planned classroom decommission of AV, and installations of new products in classrooms throughout the school

Technical Execution

The auditoriums required a detailed AV configuration, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All new devices were documented, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced site protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed their work to written specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale multi-use AV deployment for this facility. The project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination.
