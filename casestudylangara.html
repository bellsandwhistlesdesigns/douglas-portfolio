Langara College | Installations

Auditoriums and Classrooms

Overview

Led a team of 5 to upgrade current AV systems in both school auditoriums. This project required early-stage planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of complex AV systems within a short timeframe.

My Role

  • Planned and coordinated the decommissioning of legacy AV systems in auditoriums
  • Planned and coordinated the installation of monitors, drop-down projectors, speakers, microphones, and stage lighting
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and installer quality control
  • Troubleshot issues related to new system configurations

Scope of Work

  • Decommissioned and documented all AV systems from both auditoriums
  • Planned installation phases for all new systems
  • Planned and coordinated AV installations
  • Completed final installation and system integration
  • Ensured full configuration of a complex, multi-use AV system
  • Planned classroom AV decommissioning and installation of new systems throughout the school

Technical Execution

The auditoriums required detailed AV configuration, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All devices were documented to ensure accurate network integration and long-term maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced site protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication
  • Ensured all installers completed work according to specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale, multi-use AV deployments for the facility. The project was completed to a high standard, with strong documentation practices and seamless coordination.
