UBC Brock Commons South

AV Systems Integration Lead | Full Building Deployment

Overview

I led the full audiovisual deployment team for Brock Commons South at the University of British Columbia, coordinating installation of complex AV systems across multiple classrooms and learning spaces. This project required early-stage infrastructure planning, cross-trade collaboration, and precise execution and documentation of complex multi-use AV systems.

My Role

  • Lead a team of up to 8 AV installers including rotating staff and contractors
  • Planned and coordinated AV layouts for all classrooms and learning spaces
  • Oversaw full Crestron system installations
  • Managed task tracking and quality control
  • Maintained rigorous documentation standards

Scope of Work

  • 40 + displays with full Crestron control systems installed across multiple learning environments
  • Collaboration with general contractors to deliver a 4-projector, Crestron-integrated classroom
  • Extensive building pre-wiring during construction phase for all AV infrastructure
  • Room-by-room configuration of Crestron control AV systems
  • Maintain comprehensive system documentation
  • Final installation and system integration post-construction

Technical Execution

Each room required detailed AV configurations, including structured cabling, device integration, system testing and vigorous documentation. All devices were recorded ensuring accurate network integration, scalability, and long-term maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held weekly safety meetings and enforced site safety protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Maintain team morale during high-pressure siutations
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication
  • Ensured all installations met room-specific requirements
  • Maintained workflow despite evolving construction conditions
  • Resolved project challenges and delivery issues as they arose

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale AV deployment across a complex, multi-room academic facility. This challenging project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination across trades.
