UBC Brock Commons South

AV Systems Lead | Full Building Deployment

Overview

Led the full audiovisual deployment for Brock Commons South at UBC, coordinating installation across multiple classrooms and learning spaces. This project required early-stage infrastructure planning, cross-trade collaboration, and precise execution of complex AV systems.

My Role

  • Led a team of 8 AV installers with rotating staff
  • Planned and coordinated AV layouts for all classrooms
  • Oversaw full Crestron system installations
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and quality control

Scope of Work

  • 40 plus displays with full Crestron control systems installed across multiple learning environments
  • Worked with contractors to equip a 4 projector, Crestron integrated classroom
  • Extensive building pre-wiring during construction phase for all AV infrastructure
  • Final installation and system integration post-construction
  • Room-by-room configuration of Creston control AV systems

Technical Execution

Each room required a detailed AV configuration, including structured cabling, device integration, system testing and vigorous documentation. All devices were documented with serial numbers and MAC addresses, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held weekly safety meetings and enforced site protocols
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed task per room specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing construction conditions
  • Problem solving of deliverables when issues arose

Outcome

Successfully delivered a full-scale AV deployment across a complex, multi-room academic facility. The project was completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination across trades.
