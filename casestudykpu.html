Classrooms

Overview

Led multiple teams to upgrade current AV systems across various learning environments. This project required consistent planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of AV systems within a short timeframe.

My Role

  • Planned and coordinated the decommissioning of legacy AV systems in classrooms
  • Planned and coordinated the installation of new equipment across all classrooms
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and installer quality control
  • Troubleshot issues related to new system configurations

Scope of Work

  • Decommissioned and documented all existing AV systems
  • Planned installation phases for new system deployment
  • Planned and coordinated AV installations
  • Completed final installation and system integration
  • Ensured full configuration of all new AV systems across learning environments

Technical Execution

Classrooms required new AV configurations, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All devices were documented to ensure accurate network integration and long-term maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced university protocols
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed work according to specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered multiple AV deployments across the facility. Projects were completed to a high standard, with strong documentation practices and seamless coordination.
