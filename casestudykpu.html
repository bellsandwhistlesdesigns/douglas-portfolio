Classrooms

Overview

Led multiple teams to upgrade currrent AV throughout multiple learning environments. This project required consistent planning, stakeholder collaboration, and precise execution of AV systems in a short amount of time.

My Role

  • Plan and coordinate the decommission of old AV in classrooms
  • Plan and coordinate the installation of all new products back into the classrooms
  • Managed task tracking, documentation, and quality control of installers
  • Troubleshoot any issues that may arise in new product configurations

Scope of Work

  • Decommission and document all AV products from classrooms
  • Planned stages for installation of all new products
  • Planned and coordinated AV intallation
  • Final installation and system integration
  • Ensure complete configuration of every new AV system in the learning environments

Technical Execution

The classrooms required new AV configurations, including structured cabling, device integration, and system testing. All new devices were documented, ensuring accurate network integration and future maintainability.

Leadership & Coordination

  • Held safety meetings and enforced University protocols
  • Fostered a positive team environment through mentorship, support, and open communication.
  • Coordinated with stakeholders, contractors, and other trades
  • Ensured all installers completed their work to written specifications
  • Maintained workflow across changing conditions

Outcome

Successfully delivered multiple AV deployments for this facility. These projects were completed with high installation standards, strong documentation practices, and seamless coordination.
